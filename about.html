About Lacey

Hello there! I'm Lacey, and I believe that every moment has a story worth telling. Photography found me during a time when I was searching for a way to capture not just what I saw, but what I felt in those fleeting, precious moments that make life so beautiful.

My approach to photography is deeply rooted in authenticity and emotion. I'm drawn to the quiet, intimate moments between two people in love, the gentle way a mother cradles her newborn, and the genuine laughter that emerges when families are simply being themselves together.

What sets my work apart is my commitment to creating a comfortable, relaxed environment where authentic moments can unfold naturally. I believe the best photographs happen when people feel seen, valued, and completely at ease in front of the camera.

When I'm not behind the lens, you'll find me exploring new coffee shops, getting lost in a good book, or spending time in nature with my loved ones. These experiences constantly inspire my work and remind me of the beauty that exists in everyday moments.

My mission is simple: to create timeless images that tell your unique story with grace, elegance, and heart. Whether you're celebrating love, welcoming new life, or marking another beautiful chapter in your family's journey, I'm here to capture those silent stories that words alone cannot tell.
